All the Weapons and Where to Find Them

Burn off the flesh of your enemies with the Chemical Flamethrower! This weapon can set enemies on fire for massive amounts of damage, but it is only effective in short range.

1. Head to the Supplies Storage Room in the Sewers
2. Take the Queen Plug on the room below the stairs
3. Insert Queen Plug to the switch in area to the left of the stair
4. Go up the stairs and take the King Plug in the room in front of you
5. Jump down the platform on the other end of the room where you got the King Plug
6. Put the King Plug to the switch at the end of the hallway
7. Bring up the grate to get the Chemical Flamethrower
